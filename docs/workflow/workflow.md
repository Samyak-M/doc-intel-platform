### Key Process Steps for Converter & Rebranding

1. **Input:** Word-based documents, HTML, or semi-structured content
2. **Transformation:** Convert to DITA-XML; apply brand terminology mapping; clean up formatting
3. **Validation:** Verify all terms are mapped; check structure; confirm no data loss
4. **Output:** Valid DITA-XML; brand-compliant; ready for downstream publishing or CMS ingestion
